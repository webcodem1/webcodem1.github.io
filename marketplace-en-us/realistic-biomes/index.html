Realistic Biomes Add-On

The Realistic Biomes Add-On for Minecraft Bedrock offers dynamic atmospheric effects, immersive visual enhancements, and advanced customization options. It transforms each biome with realistic weather and environmental animations.

By Oreville Studios April 25, 2025

Featured Characteristics

Dynamic Weather

Includes realistic sandstorms, snowstorms, rain, and lightning effects per biome.

Enhanced Lighting & Shadows

Realistic lens flares, dynamic lighting, and customizable color correction.

Aurora Borealis & Ambient Effects

Auroras, shooting stars, and biome-specific fog for increased immersion.
